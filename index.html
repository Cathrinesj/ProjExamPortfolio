Welcome

Wanderlust is my personal blog documenting all my travels in Europe. I am currently travelling full time, and hope to inspire you for your next journey! Greece - warm and welcome. Always a taverna nearby, friendly atmosphere and some warmth to give. Spain, where the sun is (almost) always shining. UK giving us all great football and shopping - a perfect combo? All the history that each and every country has. All the amazing food and drinks, nightlife and beachlife. There really is a country, city and place for all of us. Europe has so many great places to visit, and you can read about my experience with many of them right here!
